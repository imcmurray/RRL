## Idea 1: PetPal — Pet Care Management App

**Submitted:** 2026-01-15
**Source:** Inbound inquiry from potential client
**Status:** Awaiting initial review

### Description
A comprehensive pet care management app that helps pet owners track their pets' health, schedule vet appointments, manage medications, and connect with local pet services.

### Initial Notes

- Several competitors exist (Rover, PetDesk)
- Potential for subscription revenue model
- May require integration with veterinary systems

## Idea 3: NeighborNet — Local Community Platform

**Submitted:** 2026-01-08
**Source:** Client referral
**Status:** Pending strategic fit discussion

### Description
A hyperlocal community platform for neighborhoods. Features include local events, buy/sell marketplace, service recommendations, and community announcements.

### Initial Notes
- Competes with Nextdoor and Facebook Groups
- Challenging user acquisition (network effects)
- Potentially good for B2B (HOAs, property managers)
- Need to evaluate differentiation strategy

## Evaluation Criteria

When reviewing ideas, consider:
1. **Market opportunity** — Size, growth, competition
2. **Technical feasibility** — Complexity, our expertise, timeline
3. **Business model** — Revenue potential, sustainability
4. **Strategic fit** — Alignment with our capabilities and interests
5. **Client fit** — Is this a client project or internal venture?
6. **Resource requirements** — Team availability, investment needed
